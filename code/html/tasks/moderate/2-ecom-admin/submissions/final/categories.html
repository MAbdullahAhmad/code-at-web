Discription about Category Management :

Category management in an admin panel refers to the process of organizing and managing product or content categories within a system or website. It involves creating, editing, and deleting categories to effectively categorize and structure products, services, or content for better organization and user navigation. Here is a description of category management and its key aspects :
1: Creation of Categories:
Admins can create new categories to group similar products, services, or content together. This helps users easily find what they are looking for and improves the overall user experience.

2: Editing Categories:
Admins have the flexibility to edit existing categories, including changing the category name, description, or parent category. This allows for adjustments to be made as the product or content structure evolves.

3: Hierarchy and Parent Categories:
Categories can be organized into a hierarchical structure, with parent categories containing subcategories. This hierarchy helps in organizing large sets of products or content into manageable groups.

4: Assignment of Products or Content:
Admins can assign products, services, or content to specific categories. This ensures that items are properly categorized and displayed in the relevant sections of the website or application.

5: Visibility and Display Settings:
Category management also includes settings for controlling the visibility and display of categories on the frontend. Admins can choose to show or hide certain categories based on various criteria.

6: SEO Optimization:
Proper category management is essential for SEO (Search Engine Optimization). Admins can optimize category names, descriptions, and metadata to improve search engine rankings and attract more organic traffic.
